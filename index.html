Hey there! this is SqlSheet,
SqlSheet is a Google Script Library that encourages rapid and clean development and deployment of your Database. In fact, your Google Spreadsheet is your Database. You would query data from your Google Spreadsheet using NoSql Querying paradigm. Free yourself from the hassle of Back End Web development, and Database setup and configuration. Then focus on writing your Front End Web or Mobile Application, without the need to worry about the database on a server. It’s free and open source.
Quickly have your Google Spreadsheet setup as your database. No need to lose hair over the configuration.
You might modify your google spreadsheet by sending http requests.
Focus on your mobile and Front End Web App, and let your google spreadsheet handle your data.
